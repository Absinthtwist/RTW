Day One

Wednesday 7 March 2012

Hong Kong, China

I’ve made it to my hostel intact and a bit worn out. Desperate need of a shower. That 14 hour flight is no joke. Not that I’m complaining. Could have been much worse. Seat next to me was empty and I had just the wall in front of me. Two adequate meals. 3 movies, I think, bit of a blur really.

As for Hong Kong, I have had only a glimpse. I enjoyed and can speak well of the train system, and I did walk a short block to the hostel, but I have little enough to report of the city itself. The people dress sharply, the train PA system says mind the gap just like London, but in 2 languages.

The shower is now free, I’ll talk to you all later.

Day 24: Serendipity

Friday 30 March 2012

Sihanoukville, Cambodia

Today, once again, we lounged by the water, here on Serendipity Beach, and did nothing in particular. I got a massage by the waterside, and when masseuse aggressively tried sell me a man i-pedi, I said what the hell.

She even did the thread thing where they pluck hairs and erased my uni- brow. It felt rather silly, but when in Rome…

The sun is just now setting. I can feel a little sunburn in my legs but nothing serious. Tomorrow we take a four-ish hour bus ride to Phnom Penh.

As always, a tiny bit sad to leave, but much more excited to move forward than sad.

Here’s to hoping tomorrow’s bus has A/C

Safe journey.
